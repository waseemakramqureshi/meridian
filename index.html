Welcome to Meridian Guest House


Meridian Guest House is a premium accommodation located in Kagaznagar, Telangana, near Santosh Function Hall and Minority Function Hall. We provide a comfortable and peaceful stay for guests visiting the town for family functions, business trips, or leisure travel.

Our guest house is designed to offer a relaxing environment with well-furnished rooms, modern facilities, and dedicated service. We focus on ensuring comfort, cleanliness, and convenience for every guest.

Why Choose Meridian Guest House?

  • Prime location near major function halls and town landmarks
  • Spacious and air-conditioned rooms with attached bathrooms
  • 24-hour water and electricity supply
  • Free Wi-Fi for guests

At Meridian Guest House, we believe that true hospitality lies in attention to detail and genuine care for our guests. Our mission is to deliver a pleasant and memorable stay experience at an affordable price.  Whether you are attending an event, visiting relatives, or exploring Kagaznagar, Meridian Guest House welcomes you with warmth, comfort, and quality service.
